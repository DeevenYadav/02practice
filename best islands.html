Popular Islands to Visit in India

Lakshadweep Island

Lakshadweep Island is an archipelago of 36 atolls. It lies off the coast of Kerala. However, not all islands are open for tourists; only a few are accessible with a valid permit. More than 93% of the population are Muslims. Alcohol is banned on most of the islands.

Havelock Island, Andaman

Havelock Island forms part of Ritchie’s Archipelago and is located in Andaman. It is preferred by tourists for the beaches mentioned above, water activities and watching the sunset. The island’s interior is covered with dense forests and is home to many species, such as white-headed mynas and woodpeckers.

Barren Island, Andaman

The island is located in the Andaman Sea, the only confirmed active volcano in India. The lava rocks, scuba diving and other waterworks make this tour memorable.

Majuli island

Majuli Island is the largest riverine island in the world, located on the Brahmaputra River. It is also a UNESCO site. It includes many ethnic groups, such as Mishing, Deori, Koch, Ahom, etc. The main village is Naghmar village, where festivals and other cultural activities occur.
